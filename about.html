About ThreadWorks India

Your trusted partner in creative sewing journeys

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ThreadWorks India emerged from a passion for quality craftsmanship and a vision to provide premium sewing supplies to creative minds across India. What started as a small shop in Mumbai has grown into a trusted name in the sewing community.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make quality sewing supplies accessible to everyone, from hobbyists to professional tailors. Today, we pride ourselves on offering a carefully curated selection of fabrics, tools, and accessories that meet the highest standards of quality.
